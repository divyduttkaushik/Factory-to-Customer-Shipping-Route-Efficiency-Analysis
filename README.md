# Factory-to-Customer Shipping Route Efficiency Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the shipping efficiency of Nassau Candy Distributor by evaluating factory-to-customer deliveries across different regions, states, shipping modes, and product categories.

The objective was to identify delivery bottlenecks, compare shipping performance, and evaluate profitability using interactive Power BI dashboards.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## Key KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Units Sold
- Average Lead Time
- Profit Margin %

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of sales, profitability, shipping performance, and business KPIs.

### 2. Shipping Efficiency Analysis
Analyzes delivery lead times, shipping modes, bottleneck states, and route performance.

### 3. Product & Profitability Analysis
Evaluates product sales, profitability, and division-wise performance.

---

## Key Findings

- Interior region contributed the highest sales volume.
- Shipping lead times varied across regions and states.
- Certain products generated a significant share of revenue and profit.
- Shipping performance differences highlighted opportunities for logistics optimization.

---

## Recommendations

- Improve logistics operations in high lead-time regions.
- Prioritize high-profit product categories.
- Monitor shipping mode performance.
- Use dashboard-driven KPI tracking for decision-making.
